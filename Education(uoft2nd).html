The below are the courses that I have taken during 2019 Fall. My cumulative GPA(cGPA) by then was 3.90

  1. CSC207H1 Software Design

    2. CSC207H1 Software Design

    Instructed by Professor Paul Gries

    In this course, I have learnt about different design principles and the clean architecture. I enjoyed this course a lot as we get to program our own game from scratch in Android Studio without any guidelines together as a team. Our team nailed the assignment!!! The development process was fun, especially the brainstorming part, where we get to discuss how our games look like, how we can implement different design principles. My final grade for this course is A.

  2. STA247H1 Probability with Computer Application

    3. STA247H1 Probability with Computer Application

    Instructed by Professor Karen H. Wong

    In this course, I have about counting strategies and different types of distributions such as gamma, normal, binomial and poisson. Eventhough the course materials are challenging, especially when we start to go through moment generating functions, I found the couse pretty interesting. I did the two assignments pretty well, but exam turned out to be really hard. Well, I managed to survive. My final grade for this course is A+.

  3. CSC258H1 Computer Organization

    4. CSC258H1 Computer Organization

    Instructed by Professor Maziar Goudarzi

    In this course, I have learnt about the hardware part of computers. From gates, to transistors, to processors and memory, we get to have hands on experience on these components to form useful devices and circuits. Programming in Verilog and Assembly Language is quite challenging as it is very different from programming in java and python. My final grade for this course is A-.

  4. ROP299Y1 Research Opportunity Program

    5. Research Opportunity Program (in Political Science Department)

    Supervised by Professor Lynette Ong

    This is a reserach course. In this course, I was responsible of cleaning the data and analyzing them. Every week, I read up to 100 news articles, and extract the information that we are interested in into an excel file. I wrote a simple program in python, to help me triangulate the keywords, and write it into the file, so what I have to do is to check the accuracy of the program. It was very interesting, as I had no experience in dealing with datasets this massive.
